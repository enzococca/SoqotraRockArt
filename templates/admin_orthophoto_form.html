How to prepare a COG for Dropbox:
  1. Convert your GeoTIFF to Cloud Optimized GeoTIFF format: 
    gdal_translate -of COG -co COMPRESS=JPEG input.tif output_cog.tif
  2. Upload the COG file to your Dropbox folder
  3. Enter the Dropbox path (starting with /) in the form above
  4. Optionally, get the bounding box using: 
    gdalinfo output_cog.tif

Note: Only one orthophoto should be active at a time for optimal performance.
