Soqotra Rock Art Database

Archaeological Documentation & Research Platform

About the Project

This database contains comprehensive documentation of rock art from Soqotra Island, including photographs, geospatial data, and detailed archaeological records.

The platform enables researchers to explore, analyze, and contribute to the preservation of Soqotra's archaeological heritage through an interactive mapping interface and structured data management system.
